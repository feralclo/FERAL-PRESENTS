2 Securing the Booking

The "Secured" Status: A booking is only considered "Secured" once both of the following have been completed:

  1. This contract is signed by the Promoter.
  2. The Booking Fee has been paid in full to the Brand/Agency.

Promotion Restriction: The Promoter is strictly prohibited from using the Artist's name, likeness, or branding in any public promotion (social media, flyers, website) until the booking is officially Secured.

3 Financial Terms

Artist Fee: The remaining Artist Fee must be paid no later than 30 days prior to the scheduled performance date.

Non-Payment: If the Artist Fee is not received by the due date, the Artist is not obligated to perform, and the initial Booking Fee remains non-refundable.

Artist Default: If the Artist fails to appear for the performance (excluding Force Majeure), all fees paid by the Promoter will be refunded.

4 Travel & Accommodation

Travel: The Promoter agrees to provide travel (Flights/Train/Petrol) as agreed in writing.

Ground Transport: The Promoter will provide transport between the Airport/Station, Hotel, and Venue.

Accommodation: For overnight stays, the Promoter will provide a minimum 4-star hotel room with Wi-Fi and breakfast, booked in the Artist's name.

5 Promotion & Content

Creative Freedom: Once the booking is secured, the Promoter is encouraged to be creative with promotion. The Brand/Agency will provide a press kit (photos/logos) to assist, but the Promoter is not restricted to these assets.

Recordings: The Promoter and venue are permitted to record audio or video of the performance for promotional and social media purposes.

6 Technical & Hospitality

Equipment: The Promoter agrees to provide professional-grade equipment as specified in the Technical Rider.

Catering: The Promoter will provide a "buy-out" of for a meal OR provide a quality hot meal at the venue.

Drinks: A reasonable rider of water, soft drinks, and standard alcohol will be provided in the booth during the performance.

7 Cancellation & Safety

Cancellation by Promoter: If the event is cancelled after the booking is secured, the Booking Fee is non-refundable. If cancelled within 7 days of the event, 100% of the Artist Fee is also due.

Force Majeure: Neither party is liable for failure to appear due to "Acts of God" (natural disasters, flight cancellations, or serious illness). Both parties will attempt to reschedule.

Safety: The Promoter is responsible for the safety of the Artist and their equipment while at the venue.
